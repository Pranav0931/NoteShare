# About NoteShare

NoteShare is a Flutter mobile app built for students who need a simple, trusted way to share and find academic notes inside their college community.

## What It Does

Students can sign up, choose their college details, upload notes, search study material, save useful resources, download files, review notes, and compete on a contributor leaderboard. Admin users can moderate uploaded notes before they become visible to everyone.

## Why It Exists

College study material is often scattered across chats, drives, and personal folders. NoteShare centralizes those resources into a structured academic library organized by college, branch, semester, subject, and category.

## Best For

- College students sharing class notes
- Exam preparation groups
- Communities collecting previous year papers
- Departments that want moderated academic resources
- Flutter and Supabase portfolio demonstration
